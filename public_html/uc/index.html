The Story of Sanion

Sanion was formed as part of many years of social awareness sessions, meeting millions of women across the globe, continuous R&D, product testing and above all, our vision to make our nation fully aware of menstrual hygiene management in their day to day lives. Our NGO, Centre for Hygiene Research and Development has been actively creating indepth awareness among men and women on periods, hygiene factors, choosing the right sanitary method and safe disposal of the used ones without causing harm to the ecosystem

Periods is like any natural phenomena that a human body has. It is supposed to be an absolutely normal part of the reproductive process.

But today we see women of all ages going through many difficulties physically, gynecologically and emotionally during menstruation. This is due to lack of awareness about menstrual hygiene. Sanion is our solution to all your period related problems. Sanion not only prevents such issues but also helps in curing cramps, rashes, infections and many other disorders connected to menstruation. To make such premium export quality napkins affordable to all, we chose to offer it through subscription and referral model.
